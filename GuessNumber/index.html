WELCOME TO GUESSINO

Rules of the game

- Both players input their secret number which can be from (1-9)
- Both players should enter individual bet money
- Players get alternate chances with max 5 attempts to guess opponent's secret number
- The first player to guess the opponents secret number wins!!!
- 10% of total bet money will be deducted on each attempt
- The company will get the bet money if both players fail
